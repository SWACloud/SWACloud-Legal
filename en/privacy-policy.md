# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: December 23, 2025*

## 1. Purpose and Scope

**1.1.** This Privacy Policy describes how SWACloud collects, uses, and protects your information.

**1.2.** By using SWACloud, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Data Collection

### Information You Provide

**2.1.** We collect information that you voluntarily provide:
- Account Information: Username, email address, password (hashed)
- Profile Data: Avatar, display preferences
- Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party providers

### Information Collected Automatically

**2.2.** When you use our platform, we automatically collect:
- Device Information: Hardware ID (HWID), device name, operating system
- Usage Data: Features used, timestamps, session duration
- Connection Data: IP address, browser type

**2.3.** Cloud storage files you upload are encrypted and stored securely.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for:

- Providing and maintaining our services
- Managing your account and subscription
- Processing payments and preventing fraud
- Enforcing device limits and premium features
- Improving and optimizing the platform
- Ensuring platform security

## 4. Data Storage and Security

> ℹ️ **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), secure password hashing, and access controls.

**4.1.** Your data is stored on secure servers. No method of storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Data Sharing

**5.1.** We do not sell your personal information.

**5.2.** We may share data with:
- Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate
- Legal Requirements: When required by law
- Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition

**5.3.** We never share your data with advertisers.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Request export of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support channels.

## 7. Cookies

**7.1.** We use cookies for:
- Authentication: Keeping you logged in
- Preferences: Remembering your settings
- Analytics: Understanding platform usage

## 8. Data Retention

**8.1.** We retain your data while your account is active or as needed for services.

**8.2.** After account deletion, certain data may be retained in anonymized form.

## 9. Policy Changes

**9.1.** We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
